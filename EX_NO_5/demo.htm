How is CGPA calculated?

CGPA Stands for Cumulative Grade Points Average. ... You can calculate CGPA by adding the grade points of main subjects and excluding additional subjects. Divide the sum obtained with 5 and then you get the CGPA or the grade points can be calculated by multiplying the grades and credit points.
